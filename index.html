About

Artist since early 90’s with strong passion to technology, science and art. In 2005, designed knowledge base in 3D graphics technological areas at the Intelligence Corps in the Israel Defense Forces. In 2009, focused on the gap between idea and visualization, applying CGI in architecture simulations and combined video work. After Visual Communication academic studies in Jerusalem worked on creative videos and visuals for start-ups, mobile apps, indie video games and tech companies. In 2014 moved the studio to Berlin. Working in design management, social media, web design & development and online digital services.

In 2019 began the Front-End Web Developer Nanodegree at Udacity. With a past of C++ in high-school and a free time Flash and ActionScript, going back to code after over 10 years as a designer is absolutly amazing. I'm tired of WIX and never liked Wordpress. Very exciting and fun, looking forward for the next concept chapters!
